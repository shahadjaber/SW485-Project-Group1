Original Purpose:
The dataset consists of feature vectors belonging to 12,330 user sessions. 
It was created to analyze online shopping behavior and predict whether a browsing session results in a purchase (Revenue = TRUE) or not (Revenue = FALSE). 
Each session belongs to a different user over a one-year period to avoid bias related to specific campaigns, special days, user profiles, or seasonal effects.
